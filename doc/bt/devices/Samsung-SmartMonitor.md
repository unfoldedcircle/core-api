# Samsung Smart Monitor

- Tested with M7
- Supports keyboard and mouse devices.

‼️ **work in progress**

## Known issues

- The monitor disconnects Bluetooth peripherals after power off.  
  The device cannot be turned on again, only with the original Samsung remote.
